## What Is an ECN Broker?

Before evaluating Exness, it‚Äôs crucial to understand what an **ECN broker** is. ECN stands for **Electronic Communication Network**, a system that connects traders directly to a network of liquidity providers, such as banks, hedge funds, and other traders. Unlike traditional market makers, ECN brokers:

- Offer **direct market access** without interference.
- Provide **transparent pricing** with real-time bid/ask spreads.
- Charge a **commission** per trade instead of widening spreads.
- Avoid **conflicts of interest**, as they don‚Äôt take the opposite side of client trades.

ECN brokers are favored by experienced traders for their **low spreads**, **fast execution**, and **transparency**. However, not all brokers advertising ‚ÄúECN‚Äù truly operate this way. Some use hybrid models or misleading marketing. So, where does Exness stand?
