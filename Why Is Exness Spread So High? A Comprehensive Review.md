## What Are Spreads in Forex Trading?

Before addressing why Exness spreads may seem high, letâ€™s clarify what a spread is. In forex trading, the spread is the difference between the bid price (the price at which you can sell a currency pair) and the ask price (the price at which you can buy it). This difference, measured in pips, represents the primary cost of trading and serves as a source of revenue for brokers like Exness.
